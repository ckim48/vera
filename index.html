How It Works

Behind VERA's Purpose

Earn Points with Sustainable Actions

VERA rewards you for real-world impact — not just purchases. Actions like recycling clothes, attending eco-events, and reducing waste earn you points.

  • Log your recycling and sustainability efforts.
  • Earn points verified through simple photo uploads or partner brand scans.
  • Track your impact directly through your dashboard.

Shop Only Verified Eco-Conscious Brands

Vera partners exclusively with brands that meet rigorous ethical and environmental standards.

Whether it’s recycled materials, fair labor, or net-zero shipping — every product on VERA is carefully vetted. You’ll never have to question where your fashion comes from.

Gamified Experience with Real Rewards

Make sustainability fun. Earn badges, climb leaderboards, and redeem your points for rewards that reflect your values — not just your spending.

  • Badges and milestones to track progress.
  • Rewards include exclusive discounts, ethical products, and donation options.
  • Feel good about every item you earn — it’s proof of your conscious lifestyle.
